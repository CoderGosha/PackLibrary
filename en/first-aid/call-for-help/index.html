Calling for rescue

As soon as first aid is underway, call in professional help at the first opportunity.

  1. Use any connection you have — mobile or satellite — to reach rescuers or medics.
  2. State clearly: where and when it happened, what happened, how many casualties, and what injuries they have.
  3. Learn in advance how to read exact coordinates off your phone even without internet access — it speeds up the search and the rescue.
  4. If there's no signal, send someone from the group for help, leaving whoever can keep giving first aid with the victim.
