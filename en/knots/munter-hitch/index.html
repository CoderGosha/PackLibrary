Munter hitch (UIAA / Italian hitch)

A belay/rappel friction knot tied through a carabiner — stands in for a belay device. Works in both directions and releases easily with a flick of the wrist.

  1. Fold the rope into a loop.
  2. Clip the loop onto a carabiner so both sides of the rope lie next to each other.
  3. Flip the loop over the carabiner spine — the hitch is ready; flip it again to reverse braking direction.
